Boating

Guests staying at Blue Springs Hotel can enjoy a variety of boating experiences along the picturesque coastline of Cossack. Whether exploring sheltered bays, taking a leisurely cruise across the calm waters, or admiring the region's stunning natural scenery, boating offers a relaxing way to experience the area's unique coastal environment. Both experienced boaters and beginners can find opportunities to enjoy time on the water.

Fishing

Fishing is a popular activity for visitors to Blue Springs Hotel. The waters surrounding Cossack are home to a diverse range of fish species, making them ideal for recreational fishing. Guests can cast a line from designated shoreline locations or venture further offshore for a more adventurous experience. Fishing provides a peaceful way to connect with nature while enjoying the region's beautiful surroundings.

Indigenous Awareness

Visitors to Blue Springs Hotel have the opportunity to learn about the rich Indigenous heritage of the region. Educational experiences and cultural information help guests develop a greater understanding of the traditions, history, and ongoing connection that Indigenous communities have with the land and sea. These experiences encourage respect for local culture and provide valuable insights into the area's significance.

Dining

Dining at Blue Springs Hotel offers guests a comfortable and enjoyable experience after a day of exploring. The hotel's signature venue, Blue Springs Restaurant, serves a range of delicious meals inspired by local flavours and fresh ingredients. Whether enjoying breakfast before a day of activities, a relaxed lunch, or an evening meal with family and friends, guests can experience welcoming service and quality dining in a pleasant setting.
