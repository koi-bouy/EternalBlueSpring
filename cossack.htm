About Cossack

Cossack is a historic coastal town in Western Australia's Pilbara region, known for its rich heritage and scenic waterfront. Once an important port during the late 1800s, the town played a significant role in the development of the north-west. Today, visitors can explore its well-preserved historic buildings, learn about its fascinating past, and enjoy views of the surrounding coastline. Cossack's unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty makes it a memorable destination for travellers.
